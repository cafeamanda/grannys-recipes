Homemade Tapioca Cake

Recipe by: Zenaide Moraes

  • 16+ servings
  • 55 min
  • 45 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup corn starch
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1Â½ cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Add eggs to a blender and blend well.
  2. Add oil and milk into the blender then pour tapioca flour and corn starch gently, little by little.
  3. Blend like there's no tomorrow!
  4. Add cheese into the blender and blend some more.
  5. Add baking powder and stir.
  6. Bake the dough in a greased and floured baking tray (round with a hole in the middle).
